Survival Island made in Unity

Project Description

Survival Island is a game with no real win condition. Instead it stands as a world for the player to explore and hosts two mini games on the island. It was created to increase my proficiency in unity by learning how to use all of the different features the engine offers.

Project Details

  • Battery Collecting mini game: There is a room on the island that players can only access until they collect all four batteries found on the island.
  • Coconut shooter mini game: Shoot coconuts at a target to collect a prize!
  • Features ambient sound effects and lighting such as a bright sun and tropical winds.
  • The island is very large and includes a peninsula and a volcano to explore!
