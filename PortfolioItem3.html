Super Rainbow Reef made in gamemaker

Project Description

Super Rainbow Reef is a spinoff of the brick breaker game. You control the shellfish at the bottom of the screen and bounce the starfish upwards. The point of the game is to destroy the octopi in the level while breaking as many blocks as you can.

  • The octopi can be either stationary or will move horizontally across the level
  • There are special blocks that powerup your starfish, such as spawning another starfish to hit blocks with
  • Features four unique levels
  • Has a variety of sound effects and background music
